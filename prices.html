Benefits of Privates/Duets

  • Its the best way to be introduced to the amazing benefits of the Pilates movement system.
  • Choose the times that work best for your schedule.
  • Focus on your own priorities & specific needs. This is very important if you have any special needs or injuries.
  • Get personal attention & exercises modified to suit your individual needs - making your workout safe & challenging no matter what age or stage you are at
  • Learn at your own pace instead of being tied to a group dynamic.
  • I can work with your learning style to help you create the mind/body connections to give you better physical control.
  • Use the amazing Pilates Reformer, Wunda Chair, Tower bar with Springs, Spine Corrector, Ladder barrel, Pedi-pole, OOV & Swiss balls, as well as, all the Mat Pilates small equipment such as Pilates rings/circle, bands, small balls, rollers, myofascial release balls.
