About Julie

Instructing Pilates

My passion is Pilates. I just moved to Christchurch from Wanaka where I worked for 6 years primarily at a physiotherapist Pilates studio. I worked with a diverse population with all types of injuries, conditions, & ages. some of the conditions I've worked with are Parkinson, stenosis, spondylolisthesis, trocanteric & shoulder bursitis, arthritis, pregnancy, artificial joints, herniated discs, osteoperosis, torn hamstring, shoulder impingement, hypermobility, pelvis instability, hernias, & pelvic floor issues. I also taught a senior citizens group chair based exercise with clients in their mid-70s to mid-80s, 50s plus mat classes & mat to competitive gymnasts.

I came to Pilates when I had chronic low back pain & pelvic floor issues after tearing my pelvic floor in labour with my second child. Pilates allowed me to re-connect with my body & gain core strength improving my lordotic posture which with weak core was causing low back pain.

I was so inspired with Pilates that I trained in classical Mat Pilates, a few years later I did the Stott Intensive Reformer training in Auckland, and 2015-16 I did the comprehensive Polestar Pilates studio course which focuses more on rehabilatation. I have also done workshops on back pain, hip pain, seniors, osteoperosis, pregnancy & abundant bodies.
