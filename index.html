Re:Align Pilates is a boutique Pilates studio in Westmorland. I teach small affordable Pilates mat classes of up to 6 people & Reformer & other equipment at my private studio. Such small classes allow for personal attention and I can adapt exercises to suit individual needs taking into consideration injuries, pain, or special goals of each client.

All Pilates exercises can be modified making a workout safe & challenging for a person at any level, age or stage. Pilates works for a wide range of people - all ages (teens to seniors), athletes, pregnant & postnatal women, people with limitations & injures. It helps you do better at whatever you you want to do whether it is gardening or cross training for an event. While many forms of exercise create an imbalanced body - tightening of certain muscles while overstretching others - Pilates aims to create a balanced body and is designed to cater for the needs of the individual.

Whether you want to improve your flexibility, accelerate recovery after an injury, or simply make a positive lifestyle change, ReAlign Pilates Westmorland Cashmere has something to offer you.

I have been helping people since 2010 to improve their movement and posture. I would like to do the same for you.

Polestar Fitness Screening

The fitness screening allows me to assess a client & determine their training goals & appropriate exercises to meet those goals. I require a fitness screening before starting equipment sessions. It is also useful to do before joining a mat class group. It consists of 15 tests & takes 40 minutes to an hour. It allows me to determine the following:-

  • specific & general strength
  • flexibility & coordination
  • posture & alignment
  • your starting level & hence to assess progress
  • how we need to modify the exercise to suit your individual needs & level so that you can get the most out of your classes & session
